About square dance

A square dance is a dance for four couples (eight dancers in total) arranged in a square, with one couple on each side, facing the middle of the square. It was first documented in England around the 16th centuries and after its influence through Europe it reached North America as well. The most known square dance is the Western American square dance which is associated with the american cowboys. The dance itself based on steps and figures used in traditional folk dances.

A few steps:

  • forward & back/ up to the middle and back
  • swing
  • right & left grand: going around in the circle by shaking the next partners hand (in slalom)
  • weave the ring: same as the right & left grand, but we are passing eachothers shoulders instead of shaking hands, then go back to home
  • partnet trade: face theme pass theme turn it can differ from country to country
  • tea cup chain: head ladies only!!; very difficult turns!

About the club

Today’s Square and Round Dancing is a fun activity, set to a wide variety of music that offers great social interaction while providing both physical exercise and mental stimulation.

Today’s dancers move to modern music and come from all walks of life. A pleasant mix of Download Charts, Classic Rock, Country and Latin is used at dances all around the world.
